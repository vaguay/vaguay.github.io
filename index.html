about vanesa.

I am an Ecuadorian-American high school senior in Wyomissing, PA. Passionate about using technology to reduce inequities, my passions lie in the intersection of bioenigneering and artifical intelligence where I've published neurophsyiology research conducted at Carnegie Mellon. I am also the founder of Luna Co. designing a circadian ryhthm app to improve quality of life.

I am the founder of uBridge, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit on a mission to bridge the gender and diversity gap in STEM and tech fields reaching 750+ youth with educational content in Spanish, enigneering kits, and workshops around the world. uBridge's uCoalition iniative seeks to build a global community of youth changemakers, creating a collaboration hub with youth-led iniatives from 3+ countries. I love mint chocolate chip ice-cream, winning Just Dance, and watching the Avengers.
