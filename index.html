about vanesa.

My name is Vanesa Alejandra Aguay Guerra and I'm a rising sophomore at Yale University. Passionate about using technology to reduce inequities, my passions lie in the intersection of electrical engineering, finance, and data analysis. In my free time, I love winning Just Dance, playing Ecuadorian pasillos on the piano, and coding websites like this one!

I am the author of "Hola Mundo: Inteligencia Artificial para Niños" and the founder of uBridge, a 501(c)(3) international nonprofit on a mission to bridge the diversity gap in technology fields with an entrepreuerial-thinking based philosophy. We've reached 1,200+ indigenous youth and students with educational content in Spanish, enigneering kits, and workshops around the world. Our uCoalition initiative seeks to build a global community of youth changemakers as a collaboration hub in 6+ countries. I love meeting new people so please feel free to reach out!

Contact: vanesa.aguay@yale.edu
